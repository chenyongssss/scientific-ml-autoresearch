# Rednote Research Pop

Rednote Research Pop is a visual philosophy of high-clarity attention. It should preserve the rigor of scientific work while translating it into an image that stops the scroll instantly. The composition must feel designed by someone with deep editorial judgment: bold hierarchy, immediate readability, and meticulous craft. It must look polished enough for a design-forward social platform, yet serious enough that a research audience does not feel patronized.

Color should create an attention gradient. A light, clean field establishes openness and readability, while sharply controlled dark text blocks and one confident accent color guide the eye. The result should feel painstakingly balanced, as if every line break, weight shift, and margin was refined over many iterations. Nothing should be noisy, accidental, or overdecorated. It should look expertly tuned for impact.

Scale and rhythm should do most of the communication. Large Chinese text should anchor the composition and act as the main visual mass, while smaller English and technical fragments behave like evidence markers beneath it. The hierarchy should feel masterfully composed: one glance gives the message, a second glance reveals the substance. This must read as the product of someone who has labored over proportion and pacing with exceptional care.

Composition should lean toward platform-native clarity: a strong cover image, clear top-to-bottom reading path, and bold emphasis on the central promise. Yet it should still feel like a crafted artifact, not a generic social media card. Visual accents—lines, dots, small panels, understated evidence motifs—should imply system, iteration, and intelligence without cluttering the page. Every element must feel meticulously placed.

The final work must appear as though a world-class designer with research literacy spent hours refining it for a high-signal audience. It should be immediately legible on a phone screen, but also withstand closer inspection. Crisp execution, thoughtful hierarchy, and painstaking attention to detail are essential. It must feel both social-media-ready and undeniably expert.
